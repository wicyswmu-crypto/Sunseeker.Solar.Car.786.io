About WMU Sunseeker

WMU Sunseeker is a student-led engineering organization at Western Michigan University focused on the design, development, and implementation of solar-powered systems. Our team brings together students from multiple disciplines to apply classroom knowledge to real-world renewable energy challenges.

What We Do

Sunseeker operates as a hands-on engineering team, emphasizing practical experience in system design, testing, and iteration. Members collaborate on projects involving mechanical design, electrical systems, energy management, and project planning.

⚙️ Mechanical Design
🔋 Electrical & Power Systems
📐 Engineering Analysis
🌞 Solar Energy Integration

Our Mission

Our mission is to prepare students for careers in engineering and renewable energy by providing meaningful project-based experience, fostering teamwork, and promoting sustainable innovation.

Who Can Join

WMU Sunseeker is open to students of all experience levels and majors. Whether you are new to engineering or looking to deepen your technical skills, we value curiosity, collaboration, and a willingness to learn.
