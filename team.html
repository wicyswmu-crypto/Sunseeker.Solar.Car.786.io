Competition Roles

WMU Sunseeker operates with clearly defined competition roles to ensure effective system integration, testing, and event readiness. Each role contributes directly to vehicle performance, safety, and competition success.

Vehicle Systems Lead

Oversees full vehicle integration, coordinates subsystem interfaces, and ensures compliance with competition rules and technical standards.

Electrical Systems

Responsible for solar array integration, battery management systems, wiring, power distribution, and electrical safety.

Mechanical Systems

Designs and fabricates the vehicle structure, drivetrain, suspension, and mechanical assemblies with an emphasis on reliability and efficiency.

Controls & Software

Develops data acquisition, telemetry, control logic, and system monitoring to optimize vehicle performance during testing and competition.

Testing & Validation

Conducts bench testing, system verification, fault analysis, and performance evaluation to ensure competition readiness.

Operations & Logistics

Manages timelines, documentation, transport, safety procedures, and coordination during competition events.
